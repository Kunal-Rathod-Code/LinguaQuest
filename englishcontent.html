Basic English Lessons

1. Greetings

Common English greetings:

  • Hello!
  • Hi!
  • Good Morning / Afternoon / Evening
  • How are you?
  • Nice to meet you.

2. Self Introduction

Learn how to introduce yourself:

"Hello, my name is John. I am 25 years old. I live in Mumbai."

3. Asking for Help

Useful phrases:

  • Can you help me?
  • I don't understand.
  • Could you repeat that, please?
  • What does this mean?
